#+title: How to start in a new team

0. Say hi!

1. Present yourself
  - studies
  - experiences
  - hobbies

2. Explain your role here
  - your mission
  - what they can expect of you
  - what you expect from them

3. Organize one-ones
  - what is your job here?
  - what do you like here?
  - what challenges do you see?
  - what do you expect from me?

4. Observe
  - do not juge first
  - redraw the organization chart
  - understand processes
  - note impediments
  - detect anti-patterns

5. Present your action plan
